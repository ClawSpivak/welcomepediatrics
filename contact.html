Getting Here

Directions to Our Office

We're located at 2287 Nostrand Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11210 — near Brooklyn College, between the Flatbush and Midwood neighborhoods. Street parking is available.

🚇

By Subway

Q train to Avenue J, then take the B6 or B11 bus to Nostrand Avenue.

#2 or #5 train to Flatbush Ave/Brooklyn College, then walk or take the B11 bus.

🚌

By Bus

The following MTA buses serve the area:

B6, B11, B41, B44, B49, B8, Q35

🚂

By LIRR

Take the LIRR to Flatbush Avenue station. From there, take the B41 bus north on Flatbush, or the B49 to Nostrand Avenue.

🚗

By Car — From Manhattan

Take the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway (I-278) south, then the Prospect Expressway to Ocean Avenue. Turn left on Flatbush Avenue, right on Avenue J, left on Nostrand Avenue.

🚗

By Car — From Staten Island

Take the Verrazano-Narrows Bridge to the Belt Parkway east. Exit at Flatbush Avenue north. Continue to Nostrand Avenue and turn right.

🚗

By Car — From Long Island

Take the Belt Parkway west to Flatbush Avenue north. Continue to Nostrand Avenue. Street parking is available on surrounding blocks.
