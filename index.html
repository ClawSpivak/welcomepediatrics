About Our Practice

Superior Pediatric Care Since 2006

Welcome Pediatrics PLLC was founded in 2006 by two experienced pediatricians committed to providing exceptional care for children and young adults in Brooklyn.

👶

Infants Through Age 21

We accept new patients from newborns through age 21. Prenatal visits are offered free of charge to expecting parents.

🩺

Board-Certified Pediatricians

Dr. Svetlana Spivak and Dr. Irina Nelipovich are both board-certified Fellows of the American Academy of Pediatrics with 30+ years each.

🏥

Pediatrics-Only Facility

Our office is dedicated exclusively to pediatric care — a comfortable, child-friendly environment where kids and parents feel at home.

📋

Comprehensive Care

Well-child visits, immunizations, diagnostic testing, asthma management, weight counseling, and more — all under one roof.

Our Physicians

Experienced, Caring Pediatricians

Our founders bring decades of experience and a genuine love for their patients and the Brooklyn community.

Dr. Svetlana Spivak, M.D., FAAP

Co-Founder & Pediatrician

Practicing pediatrics for over 30 years, Dr. Spivak trained at Leningrad Medical School and completed her residency at Elmhurst Hospital (Mount Sinai). She has served Brooklyn families for 25 years and is a Fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics.

Read full bio

Dr. Irina Nelipovich, M.D., FAAP

Co-Founder & Pediatrician

Dr. Nelipovich graduated from Minsk State Medical School and completed her Mount Sinai residency before partnering with Dr. Spivak to found Welcome Pediatrics in 2006. She has over 30 years of pediatric experience and is a Fellow of the AAP.
